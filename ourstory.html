The BookEx Story

Project Abstract

Every term, many students are burdened with finding and acquiring course textbooks. At the same time, other students have many used textbooks sitting in storage that others would like to borrow. A survey showed 68% of people who purchase and keep textbooks would loan them to friends. BookEx is a web based application that facilitates the exchange of books between two people with valid UW NetIDs. It allows users to search by title, ISBN, and author for books that others are willing to loan. The borrowing and lending process in BookEx allows users to accept or deny books requests and tracks who borrowed their books. We used a combination of surveys, interviews, prototyping and user testing, which guided our full implementation of the BookEx application.
